Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and password. We also collect the financial information you choose to enter, including expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to operate core features of Spendly, such as displaying your expense history, generating category breakdowns, and producing monthly spending summaries. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are accessible only to you through your authenticated account. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing and are never kept in plain text.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. Where third-party services are used (for example, for hosting or infrastructure), they are bound to handle data only as needed to provide the service to Spendly.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at support@spendly.app.
